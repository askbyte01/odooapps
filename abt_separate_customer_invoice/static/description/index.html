Separate Customer Invoice

Features

Custom Action "Separate Customer Invoice":

  • You've added a custom action within the sale order model interface, which users can interact with to initiate specific actions related to invoicing.

    • Separate Invoice Creation:

  • When users click on the "Separate Customer Invoice" action, it triggers the creation of an invoice for the corresponding sale order.
  • Unlike the standard flow where invoices are created automatically or in batches, this action allows users to initiate the creation of an invoice individually and separately from the usual process.

    • Customization Purpose:

  • This customization might be useful for scenarios where users need more control over the timing or specifics of invoice generation. It can cater to specific business requirements or workflows that deviate from the standard Odoo behavior.
